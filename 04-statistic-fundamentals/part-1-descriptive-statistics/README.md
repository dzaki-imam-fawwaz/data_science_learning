# Part 1 - Descriptive Statistics

Descriptive statistics helps us summarize and understand a dataset.

## Main Questions

| Concept | Question Answered |
|---|---|
| Mean | What is the average value? |
| Median | What is the middle value? |
| Mode | What value appears most often? |
| Range | How far is the maximum from the minimum? |
| Variance | How spread out is the data? |
| Standard Deviation | On average, how far are values from the mean? |

## Why This Matters

Before building machine learning models, you need to understand your data.

For example:

- Average customer spending
- Salary distribution
- Student score consistency
- Sales performance
- Model error spread

## Key Idea

Mean tells us the center of the data.

Standard deviation tells us how messy or spread out the data is.
